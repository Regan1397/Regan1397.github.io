Our Brand

WeSeeYou is an exploration into the publication of surveillance and security cameras within organistions and goverment authorities
The website searches security camer footage within a certain area and time frame, attempting to find the user within the footage.
The website then saves the video footage that contains the user, and creates a graph like webpage showing where the user has been while under security camera footage and how long they were present - this information is at the users disposal.

How it Works

The website creates a digital representatin of the users facial structure by creating algorthims accurately coping their facial structure, this is then tracked by using the users built in Webcam (With the users permission). The websites processing syste then checks security cameras within a given area or time frame, searching for the users presence within this footage and saves that footage. This project isn't 100% feasible within this present time, but with the steady development of technology i belive this project will be possible within this decade.

Our Objectives

The purpose of WeSeeyou and this projrct is to identify and emphasise the fact our human right ot privacy is being diminished, reducing our individual freedom and democracy. Advancing technology has given goverment insitutions far too much power over the individual. We need to put a stop to this, now. WeSeeYou's main objective is to inform the public on a wide scale showing the power that the goverment has over us.
